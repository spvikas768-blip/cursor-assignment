# AI-Powered SEO Content Production Research

## Overview

This repository contains research on respected practitioners in AI-powered SEO content production. The goal is to identify industry experts whose work can be used as reliable references for future research and content creation.

## Why This Topic

AI has significantly changed SEO and content production. Modern SEO professionals increasingly rely on AI-assisted workflows, semantic search, topical authority, and content optimization platforms. Researching leading experts provides trustworthy learning resources and current best practices.

## Why These Experts Were Selected

The selected practitioners were chosen because they:

- Have established credibility in SEO.
- Publish educational content regularly.
- Work at respected SEO companies or run successful independent consultancies.
- Focus on AI-assisted content creation, semantic SEO, search quality, and modern optimization techniques.
- Are frequently referenced within the SEO community.
